Timothy Jordan is the creator of a14y. He believes in solving problems for the
benefit of humankind, and that the most interesting problems are solved with
scaled solutions by the global developer community. He has over two decades of
experience working at the intersection of engineering and storytelling to help
developers be more effective, and is passionate about Open Source, Developer
Ecosystem, and the search for the ideal developer journey. Previously: VP of
Developer Experience at Vercel, and ~16 years at Google leading Developer
Relations for Flutter, Firebase, Angular, Dart, TensorFlow, Maps, Workspace,
and Material.
